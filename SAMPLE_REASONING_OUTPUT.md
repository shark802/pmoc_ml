## Key Features

1. **Conflict Detection**: Automatically detects when risk level conflicts with response-based indicators
2. **Clear Explanation**: Explains why ML model prediction overrides response-based calculation (or vice versa)
3. **Demographic Analysis**: Detailed breakdown of age gap, civil status, education-income compatibility, employment
4. **Response-Based Analysis**: Alignment score, conflict ratio, and category-specific alignments
5. **Decision Source**: Shows which method (response-based vs ML model) was used and why
6. **Summary**: Concise summary of all factors contributing to the risk classification
